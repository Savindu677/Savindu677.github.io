Frequently Asked Questions

What is 4K Cinema?

4K cinema offers ultra-high-definition resolution, providing four times the pixel resolution of Full HD for an immersive experience.

How do I book tickets for movies?

You can easily book tickets through our website or mobile app by selecting the movie, time, and number of seats.

What is the seating arrangement in the cinema?

Our cinema provides luxurious recliner seating with ample legroom and adjustable positions for maximum comfort during the movie.

Can I bring outside food to the cinema?

For health and safety reasons, we kindly ask that you enjoy our delicious food offerings at the cinema. We have a variety of snacks and drinks available for purchase.
